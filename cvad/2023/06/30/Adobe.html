Educational Institution Subscriptions

Below are some of the details regarding the availability of Adobe Creative Cloud subscriptions for University of North Texas employees and students. If you are a student who is considering purchasing or has purchased a subscription through our webstore offer, please review the full terms and conditions that are provided there as well: UNT Software Webstore.

  • Full-time employees receive a full educational institution subscription as part of their employment. When an employee leaves UNT, they lose access to their account and will no longer be able to access the applications or files stored on the Adobe Cloud from any device. Before an employeeâ€™s final day employed by UNT, they should discuss migrating the data stored on the Adobe Cloud with their supervisor for business continuity purposes. Former employees will not be permitted access to their accounts, or the files stored on the Adobe Cloud associated with those accounts. The accounts are provided as part of their employment and the data is the property of the institution.
  • Part-time employees who require Adobe Creative Cloud to complete job-related duties should contact their department regarding a subscription.
  • Students have access to the Adobe Creative Cloud applications while they are working physically on campus in one of our computer labs or classrooms at no additional cost. This is granted through a shared device license and there are limitations on this license: only select applications are available, none of the online features are available (this includes Adobe Fonts and Adobe Stock Images). Additionally, home-use, remote access, and access using a personal device are not available when using a shared device license.
  • Students who wish to use the Adobe Creative Cloud applications on a UNT checkout laptop, the UNT Online Computer Lab, or on their personal devices will need to purchase a personal subscription. UNT offers heavily discounted educational institution subscriptions for students: $55.55 per academic year. For more information, full terms, and conditions, and to purchase a subscription, please visit our webstore. Students must remain actively enrolled at UNT in order to access their account as these are educational subscriptions.
  • Neither the UNT System nor UNT campus agreements provide personal subscriptions of any type. We provide educational enterprise subscriptions. This is important as there are key differences between these two types of subscriptions and it can be confusing when trying to determine how to resolve issues that can arise because those differences are not always made very clear.
  • If you are a student, with an existing personal subscription with Adobe and you used your UNT email address to sign up for that account: PLEASE change the email address associated with that account before you purchase a subscription through the UNT offer. You can do that by following these instructions: https://helpx.adobe.com/sign/using/change-email-address.html. You cannot change the email address on the accounts available through our offer. Adobe technical support does not appear capable of distinguishing between accounts with the same email address. It will greatly hinder you when you need help from our office or from Adobe if you have two accounts with the same email address.

Logging into your Educational Institution Account

Regardless of whether you are a full-time employee who received a subscription as part of your employment, a student who is using the applications while working in an on-campus computer lab or classroom, or a student who has purchased a full subscription through our webstore: the steps below will walk you through how to access your account

The images below show the steps to login to Adobe Creative Cloud:

Step 1: Initial Login Screen

All UNT subscriptions are tied to a UNT email address, and the email address cannot be changed. You must login using your UNT email address.

Which email address do you use when logging in?
  • Employees: @unt.edu
  • Students: @my.unt.edu


Step 2: Select Account Type

When prompted during login, select Company or School Account. If you try to login with a non-UNT email address, or select Personal Account, the login will fail as these are educational institution subscriptions, not personal subscriptions.

Step 3: UNT Single Sign-On

You will need to enter your EUID and password. If you need to reset your password or do not know your account credentials, please visit the UNT Account Management website.

Step 4: Profile Selection

The screen shown to your left is where you are prompted to choose a profile: students who purchased a subscription from us should choose the profile that reads UNIVERSITY OF NORTH TEXAS. All others (faculty, staff and students using the software in a classroom or computer lab on-campus) should choose the one that reads UNIV. OF NORTH TEXAS. This screen does not appear in all cases.

Addtional Information on Adobe Creative Cloud Student Subscriptions:

It may take up to two business days for your Adobe CC license to be activated. If after two days, you are not able to login: please contact us at adobe@unt.edu and please provide your order number and the date of purchase.

To login to your Adobe account launch the Adobe Creative Cloud desktop application on any computer that has the Adobe Creative Cloud applications installed or go to the Adobe Creative Cloud web portal. You will have the option to install the software on a personal computer or device through the web portal.

If you already have a subscription to the Adobe Creative Cloud but would like to take advantage of this offer: Adobe will allow you to opt-out of your existing agreement, without incurring an early termination fee once you have purchased a subscription through this offer. You must contact Adobe within 14 days of purchase to opt-out without penalty.

Adobe Customer Care can be reached by phone at 1-800-833-6687, you may also visit the Adobe Customer Support Website and talk to someone via chat.

This is an educational subscription. To maintain your access to the software and services provided by the subscription you must remain actively enrolled as a student at the institution. The subscription will expire on August 31st unless your status as a student at UNT changes before this date. If your status as an active student changes your subscription will be terminated at that time. If you purchase a subscription during the Fall semester and do not enroll in at least one course at UNT during the Spring semester your subscription will be terminated on or after the 12th class day of the semester. If you withdraw from UNT at any point prior to August 31st, your subscription will be terminated at that time. If you remain enrolled at UNT through the Spring semester, regardless of whether you enroll in summer courses, your subscription will remain active until August 31st.

We are not able to offer prorated prices or refunds.

When you graduate or leave UNT you may offboard your account to Adobe. After offboarding, your files will be transferred to a free Adobe-hosted personal account. Personal accounts are not eligible for any UNT-negotiated discounts so your subscription will end at that time. While the personal Adobe accounts are free, you must purchase a subscription to use the applications. If you are leaving UNT and are not ready to start a new subscription with Adobe right away, you can still offboard to preserve your files in the cloud. For more information, please see the Adobe website on Student Asset Migration.

Please contact us at adobe@unt.edu with any questions.
